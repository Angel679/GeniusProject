How to Draw a Wereworlf

Step 1

Begin by creating the basis to ensure that your anatomy is correct.

Step 2

Using the circle that you have created, form a sort of dog head. Afterwards, create both arms using the basis to guide you

Step 3

Using the basis, create both the inner part of the wereworlf and the legs. Dont forget to draw the neck

Step 4

Afterwards, draw both the legs and tail. Id advise to space everything out in your mind so to keep the anatomy in check (you could erase the basis if youd like)

Step 5

Spacing the chest area, draw some details such as the bellybutton. The hands however, may be a difficult for some and if you wish you could do them later. Id advise to space everything out to keep your anatomy in check (Its really helpful!)

Step 6

Lastly, draw the face and the toenails of the wereworlf
